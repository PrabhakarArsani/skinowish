Autologous GFC Kit

Advanced Regenerative Therapy for Optimal Results

What is Autologous GFC Kit?

  • GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) kit is an advanced regenerative therapy prepared from the patient's own blood (autologous = self-derived).
  • The kit helps isolate and concentrate growth factors and beneficial proteins naturally present in the blood.
  • These growth factors stimulate cell repair, tissue regeneration, and healing.

Benefits to Patients

100% Safe & Natural

Since it uses the patient's own blood, there is no risk of allergy or rejection.

Faster Healing & Recovery

Growth factors accelerate tissue repair, reduce downtime, and improve recovery in treatments like hair restoration, skin rejuvenation, and orthopedic healing.

Effective for Hair & Skin Treatments

Promotes new hair growth, strengthens existing hair, and enhances skin texture, tone, and glow.

Minimally Invasive

Simple procedure involving a small blood draw and injection, with minimal discomfort.

Long-Lasting Results

Stimulates the body's natural regenerative process, ensuring more sustained improvement compared to temporary treatments.

Summary

Autologous GFC Kit = Safe, Natural, and Effective regenerative therapy that heals, restores, and rejuvenates using the patient's own blood.
